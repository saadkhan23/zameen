### Option B: Buy Plot and Build

**What You Pay:**
- Plot price (from your scraped data)
- Construction cost (current rate ~Rs. 3,000-4,500 per sq ft)
- Architect/designer fees (2-5% of construction)

- Landscaping

**Example Calculation:**

```

- Supervision: PKR 2 Lakh

- Landscaping: PKR 3 Lakh

Total Construction: PKR 75 Lakh
```
